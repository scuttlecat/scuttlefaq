If you got it from loot, **NO.** The only skins that could be considered 'rare' are ones that were only available long ago and are not in the loot pool. Examples: Black Alistar, Silver Kayle, Riot Squad Singed. All skins have the same drop chance from loot except mythic skins (which are still not rare because they're in loot and the mythic shop.)
